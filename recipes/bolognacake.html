Bologna Cake

Description:

This has everything nobody would ever want in a cake! It might be edible, but your palate would disagree. Treat yourself and your loved ones and watch their faces as you subject them to this faustian culinary experience.

Ingredients: (serves 6-8)

  • 2 pkg. (8 oz.) softened cream cheese
  • 1 pkg. (1 oz.) Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing
  • 12 oz. (weight) Oscar Mayer bologna
  • 1 can (8 oz.) sharp cheddar aerosol cheese
  • 1 whole sprig of cilantro or pimento-stuffed olives (for garnish)
  • 1 pkg. buttery crackers, (Townhouse or Ritz)

Procedure: (approx. 15 mins)

  1. Mix ranch dressing into softened cream cheese, set aside.
  2. Place one slice of bologna on a plate and frost with 1 tbsp of cream cheese mixture.
  3. Repeat, stacking slices on top of each other to form the cake.
  4. Use any remaining cream cheese to frost the outside of the cake.
  5. Pipe cake flowers with aerosol cheese around the bottom and top of the cake to decorate.
  6. Finish decorating the top with the sprig of cilantro, or pimento-stuffed olives.
  7. When serving, cut a small slice and garnish with a wedge of buttery cracker.
  8. Try to enjoy!
